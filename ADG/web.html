ABOUT

    Approximately 90% of the world's population is right-handed.
It's All Right to be the 10% left.

    ‘All Right’ is a campaign aiming to call for more attention
to left-handed people, who contribute to approximately
10% of the world’s population.
    By demonstrating 10 left-handed items, ‘All Right’
encourages more lefty-friendly products that will make
life easier for the community to be manufactured.
